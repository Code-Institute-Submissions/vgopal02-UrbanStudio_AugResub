PILATES


Pilates exercises are performed in a flow of movement unlike yoga.

Pilates classes offered include Classical, Mat, Contemporary and Stott Pilates.


Types of pilates


  Classical

In Classical Pilates method there is a sequence designed to move the body through a full range of movement.


   Mat - Intermediate

Mat Pilates is a pilates training practice that can be done on a mat. In this class, you hold movements like planks to build your core strength.


  Contemporary - All levels

It incorporates modern knowledge of the body and biomechanics into Pilates.


  Stott Pilates - All levels

Stott Pilates is a contemporary version of the Classical Pilates method.

MEDITATION


When we meditate, we inject far-reaching and long-lasting benefits into our lives.

Meditation classes offered Beginners, Walking and Body Scan Meditation.


Types of Meditation


  Beginners Meditation

You are taught about the breathe and how to control it through simple techniques.


   Walking Meditation

For those who prefer movement


  Body Scan Meditation

Body scan meditation is a good way to release tension you might not even realize you're experiencing. Body scanning involves paying attention to parts of the body and bodily sensations in a gradual sequence from feet to head
